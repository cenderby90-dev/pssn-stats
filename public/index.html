How Champions of Shame Rankings Work
Event Quality Multiplier
Not all events are equal. A win at a 1,000-player GT carries more weight than a win at a 24-player club RTT. We scale each event using log(players) / log(100) — so a 100-player event is 1.0×, a 1,000-player event is ~1.5×, and a 24-player event is ~0.7×.
Bayesian Win Rate
Raw win rate rewards players who play fewer, carefully chosen events. We use a Bayesian adjustment to pull small samples toward the team average — the more you play, the more your actual rate matters.
Provisional Threshold
Players who have attended fewer than 2 events are listed as provisional. A single strong performance isn't enough to earn a ranked position.
Teams Events
Teams event results are excluded from the Singles, GT and RTT tabs. They appear only in the Teams tab and in each player's individual event history.
